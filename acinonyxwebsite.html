ABOUT ACINONYX

ACINONYX is a collaboration STEM Racing team between Apex Force of MRSM Taiping and Rapidgrine of Sekolah Tun Fatimah.

The team name, Acinonyx is inspired by the fastest mammal in the world, the cheetah. It represents agility, focus and the unyielding pursuit of excellence, the core values of our team.

OUR LOGO

Our logo was designed to represent more of the team's core values while still keeping the cheetah influence in mind.

The teardrop-shaped line extending from the inner corner of the eye symbolises team's focus on racing and completing numerous tasks.

A noticible element of our team design, the gradient dot, is an homage towards the cheetah's iconic spots, while also representing the team's flexibility.

COLOUR SCHEME

We have chosen three colours for the team's branding, each holding their own meaning.

Grey is deemed as a professional colour. A combination of two, very contrasting colours (black and white), it symbolises adaptability and balance of work in our team.

Yellow is often referred as a colour with energy. This bright colour was chosen to catch the eyes of the audience.

Last but not least, our dominant colour, Dark Teal conveys stability and strength, adding a touch of mystery and elegance while still maintaning an air of energy and focus.

OUR MOTTO

For Acinonyx, this slogan captures the drive to break through technical and personal barriers, like a cheetah exceeding it's limits in speed. It reminds the team to always push ourselves to be the best.
